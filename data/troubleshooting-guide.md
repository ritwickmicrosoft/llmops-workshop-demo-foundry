# Wall-E Electronics Troubleshooting Guide

## Common Issues and Solutions

### Device Won't Turn On

1. **Check the battery**: Charge for at least 30 minutes before attempting to power on
2. **Force restart**: Hold power button for 15 seconds
3. **Check charging cable**: Try a different cable/adapter
4. **Look for damage**: Check for visible damage to ports or buttons

### Slow Performance

1. **Restart device**: Simple restart fixes most issues
2. **Free up storage**: Delete unused apps and files (keep 20% free)
3. **Update software**: Settings → System → Software Update
4. **Factory reset**: Last resort - backup data first!

### Connectivity Issues (Wi-Fi/Bluetooth)

1. **Toggle off/on**: Turn wireless off, wait 10 seconds, turn back on
2. **Forget and reconnect**: Remove saved network and rejoin
3. **Reset network settings**: Settings → Network → Reset
4. **Check router**: Restart your home router

### Battery Draining Fast

1. **Check battery health**: Settings → Battery → Health
2. **Reduce brightness**: Enable auto-brightness
3. **Close background apps**: Double-tap home to view and close
4. **Disable unused features**: Location, Bluetooth when not needed

### Audio Problems

1. **Check volume**: Ensure not muted or on vibrate
2. **Clean speakers**: Use soft brush to remove debris
3. **Test with headphones**: Isolate if speaker or software issue
4. **Restart**: Clears audio processing issues
