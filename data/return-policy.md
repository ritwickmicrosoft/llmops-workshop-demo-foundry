# Wall-E Electronics Return Policy

## 30-Day Satisfaction Guarantee

We want you to love your Wall-E products! If you're not completely satisfied, you can return most items within 30 days of delivery for a full refund.

## Return Eligibility

**Eligible for Return:**
- Unopened products in original packaging
- Products with manufacturing defects
- Items that don't match the description

**Not Eligible:**
- Products damaged by misuse or accidents
- Items without proof of purchase
- Software/digital downloads after activation
- Customized or personalized items

## How to Return

1. **Initiate Return**: Log into your Wall-E account → Orders → Request Return
2. **Print Label**: Download the prepaid shipping label (free for defective items)
3. **Pack Securely**: Use original packaging if available
4. **Ship**: Drop off at any carrier location within 7 days

## Refund Timeline

- **Original payment method**: 5-7 business days after we receive the item
- **Wall-E Store Credit**: Instant (bonus 10% for choosing credit)
