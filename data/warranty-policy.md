# Wall-E Electronics Warranty Policy

## Standard Warranty Coverage

All Wall-E products come with a **2-year limited warranty** covering:
- Manufacturing defects
- Hardware malfunctions under normal use
- Battery degradation below 80% capacity

## Extended Warranty (Wall-E Care+)

Upgrade to Wall-E Care+ for enhanced protection:

| Plan | Duration | Coverage | Price |
|------|----------|----------|-------|
| Care+ Basic | 3 years | Defects + 1 accidental damage | $99 |
| Care+ Pro | 4 years | Defects + 2 accidental damage + theft | $149 |

## What's NOT Covered

- Physical damage from drops, spills, or misuse
- Cosmetic damage (scratches, dents)
- Damage from unauthorized repairs
- Software issues or virus damage
- Lost or stolen items (unless Care+ Pro)
