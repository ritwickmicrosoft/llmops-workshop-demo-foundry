# Wall-E Electronics Shipping Policy

## Shipping Options

| Method | Delivery Time | Cost | Free Threshold |
|--------|---------------|------|----------------|
| Standard | 5-7 business days | $5.99 | Orders $50+ |
| Express | 2-3 business days | $12.99 | Orders $150+ |
| Next Day | 1 business day | $24.99 | Never free |
| Same Day | Same day (select cities) | $34.99 | Never free |

## Free Shipping

- **Standard**: Free on orders $50 and above
- **Express**: Free on orders $150 and above
- **Members**: Wall-E Plus members get free express on all orders

## Tracking Your Order

1. Order confirmation email includes tracking number
2. Track at wall-e.com/track or in the Wall-E app
3. SMS updates available (opt-in at checkout)

## International Shipping

We ship to 40+ countries. International orders:
- Delivery: 7-14 business days
- Duties/taxes: Customer responsibility
- Returns: Buyer pays return shipping

## Delivery Issues

**Package not received?**
- Standard: File claim after 10 business days
- Express/Next Day: File claim after 3 business days
- Contact: shipping@wall-e.com
